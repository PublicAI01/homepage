## Staking Hero Challenge is back for another Season!

### How it works:

- Stake **5,000+ PUBLIC** at [token.publicai.io/stake](https://token.publicai.io/stake)
- Every **5,000 PUBLIC staked = 1 lottery ticket per day**
- Daily tickets adjust with your staking balance at snapshot time

**Example:**

- **Day 1:** Balance = 5K → **1 ticket**
- **Day 2:** Balance = 15K → **3 tickets**
- **Day 3:** Balance = 10K → **2 tickets**

At the end of the week, **1 winner** is randomly selected to take home the full **$5,000 USDT prize pool**.

### Why join?

One stake. Daily chances. Your tickets scale with your balance, and your stake keeps earning passively while you play for $5,000.
